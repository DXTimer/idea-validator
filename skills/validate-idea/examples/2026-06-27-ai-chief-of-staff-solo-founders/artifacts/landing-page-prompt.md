# Landing page prompt — paste into v0 / Lovable / Bolt / Stitch

This builds the smoke-test / pre-sale page for [The Saved Placement Pilot](../offer.md).
Use it as a secondary demand signal alongside the paid concierge; the deposit is the
real test. Paste everything below the line.

---

Build a single-page, conversion-focused landing page for a paid pilot service aimed at
**solo technical recruiters and 1-3 person boutique staffing agency owners**. Tone:
sharp, direct, a little blunt, built by an operator for operators. Not corporate, no
stock-photo gloss. Fast-loading, mobile-first, one clear call to action repeated 3
times. Dark or high-contrast theme, big confident type, generous spacing.

**Hero**
- Headline: "Never let a $25,000 placement die in your inbox again."
- Subhead: "For 14 days, a real person watches your email, calendar, LinkedIn, and Slack and tells you every high-stakes follow-up that's about to slip. You pay $500. If we don't catch one worth more than that, it's free."
- Primary CTA button: "Claim a pilot seat ($500, catch-one-or-free)" → opens a Stripe checkout / deposit form.
- Trust line under the button: "Read-only access. NDA before we start. 5 seats at a time."

**Problem section** ("You already know this pain")
Three short cards:
1. "A finalist went quiet for three days and took the other offer."
2. "A client req sat untouched while you were buried in sourcing."
3. "You found the perfect candidate and forgot to follow up until it was cold."
Caption: "Every dropped follow-up is a five-figure fee you already earned and let walk."

**How it works** (3 steps, numbered)
1. "20-minute setup call. We connect read-only to your inbox, calendar, LinkedIn, and Slack. You do nothing."
2. "Day 1: we hand you every stalled follow-up from your last 30 days. Found money before lunch."
3. "Every morning: a short 'About to Slip' list of the 3-7 follow-ups quietly dying right now. You send them. Deals stop leaking."

**The offer / value stack** (a boxed, anchored offer)
- "14-day done-for-you follow-up safety net" — the core
- "Bonus: Day-1 Pipeline Triage (every rotting thread from the last 30 days)"
- "Bonus: White-glove 20-minute setup"
- "Bonus: End-of-pilot Saved Deals Report — every catch, the fee at risk, and where your desk leaks revenue"
- Guarantee badge: "Catch one or it's free. If we don't surface a follow-up worth more than $500, you pay nothing and keep the report."
- Price line: "$500 today. First 10 founders lock $49/mo for life (list $99-149) and turn $500 into $1,000 of launch credit."

**Objection killers** (FAQ accordion)
- "Doesn't my CRM already do this?" → "Your CRM reminds you about the records you remembered to enter. This catches the ones scattered across email, LinkedIn, and Slack that never made it into the CRM."
- "Is it secure?" → "Read-only access, NDA signed first, revoke any time. We never send anything as you."
- "I don't have time to set up another tool." → "There is no tool. A person sets it up in 20 minutes and does the watching."
- "What if it doesn't catch anything?" → "Then it's free. That's the whole deal."

**Scarcity / urgency**
A small live-feeling counter: "Pilot seats this cohort: 5. Founding seats left: ___."
Deadline line: "Founding pricing closes Friday."

**Final CTA**
Repeat the primary button and the guarantee line.

**Form fields for the deposit / waitlist**: name, work email, agency name, rough number
of live candidates, and "what's the most recent deal you lost to a dropped follow-up?"
(free text — this last field is gold for the interviews).

Make the single conversion action unmistakable. Everything points to claiming a seat.
