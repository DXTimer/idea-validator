# Cold outreach — recruit the first 20 pilots

Goal: get 50 qualified solo recruiters in front of [The Saved Placement Pilot](../offer.md)
and see how many pay the $500 deposit. The kill number is 7. One ask, no fluff.

## Where to find them
- LinkedIn search: title "Technical Recruiter" OR "Talent Partner" + "Founder" / "Owner"
  at 1-5 person agencies. Independents, not in-house.
- Communities: RecOps Collective, SourceCon, The Recruiting Lab, RecruitingBlogs,
  r/recruiting, independent-recruiter Slack/Facebook groups.

## LinkedIn DM (the opener)
> Quick one. For the next 2 weeks I'll personally watch your inbox, calendar, and
> LinkedIn and ping you the moment a hot candidate or client follow-up is about to slip.
> First 10 founding recruiters get it for $500, catch-one-or-it's-free, and lock $49/mo
> for life. I've got 2 of 5 pilot slots open this week. Want one?

## Cold email (slightly longer)
Subject: the $25k follow-up you're about to drop

> Hi {first name} — you run your desk solo, so you already know the problem: a finalist
> goes quiet, a client req sits, and a five-figure fee you already earned walks out the
> door.
>
> I'm running a 2-week pilot for 5 recruiters at a time. I personally watch your email,
> calendar, LinkedIn, and Slack and send you one daily "about to slip" list of the
> follow-ups quietly dying. Day one I dig out every stalled thread from your last 30
> days, so you get found money immediately.
>
> $500 for the two weeks. If I don't catch a single follow-up worth more than that, you
> pay nothing and keep the report. Read-only access, NDA first, cancel anytime.
>
> First 10 founders lock $49/mo for life when the software ships and turn the $500 into
> $1,000 of credit. Want one of this week's slots?
>
> {your name}

## Tracking (so the test is real)
Log every send in a sheet: name, channel, sent date, replied?, booked setup call?,
**paid deposit?**, and the one-line reason for any no. The no-reasons are as valuable as
the yeses — they tell you whether the wedge is wrong or just the pitch. Read the result
against the kill number in [the test](../riskiest-assumption-and-test.md): under 7 paid
deposits out of 50 warm, qualified prospects → the detection wedge is dead, pivot.
